Why Bilbao?

Bilbao, the city of design

Bilbao, known as "The City of the Guggenheim" and a UNESCO City of Design, has transformed from an industrial hub into a cultural and creative center, boasting world-class museums, theaters, and a vibrant arts scene, while also preserving its traditional heritage. The iconic Guggenheim Museum, designed by Frank Gehry, symbolizes the city's modern renaissance and global appeal. Steeped in Basque traditions, Bilbao offers a unique cultural experience with the Euskara language widely spoken and visible on street signs.
